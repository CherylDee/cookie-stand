So Glad You Stopped By!

Welcome to the place to learn about the passion that goes into the making of Pat's Gourmet Salmon Cookies. These sweets are not only delicious, but they are made with love for the environment and maintaining healthy ecosystems. When you buy healthy salmon cookies you are helping to bring awareness of the environment and the issues of climate change, as well as the devastation that comes along with it. Please enjoy these goodies and we'd love to hear from you! Salmon Cookies can be enjoyed guilt-free by humans and all sentient life forms!
